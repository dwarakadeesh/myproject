The Ramoji Film City in India is located in Hyderabad. At 2000 acres, it is the largest integrated film city in Telangana and the largest studio complex in the world. The film city was also certified by the Guinness World Records as the world’s largest Film Studio complex. It was set up by Ramoji Group in 1996.
